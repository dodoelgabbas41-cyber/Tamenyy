Why Early Screening Matters?

In Egypt, non-communicable diseases are the leading cause of mortality. Most can be managed if caught early.

It's Often Silent

Hypertension is called the "Silent Killer" because it rarely shows symptoms until it's too late.

Saves You Money

Treating a chronic condition in late stages costs 10x more than preventative care and simple diet changes.

Protects Family

Genetics play a huge role. Knowing your status helps protect your children's future health choices.

Common Questions

Is the screening really free?
Yes. Our partner clinics offer specific hours (Tameny Free Hours) where basic screenings for Blood Pressure and Random Blood Sugar are 100% free.
Is my data shared with the government?
No. Tameny is an independent non-profit. The risk assessment happens on your phone, and we do not store your name or National ID.
Can I book a full doctor consultation?
The free service covers screening. If issues are found, our partners offer discounted rates for full consultations, but that is optional.
